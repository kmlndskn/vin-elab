Given the comprehensiveness of entrepreneurship mindset and skills, E@V framework can simultaneously support the development of all five VinUni graduate attributes.

In particular, through the E@V framework, we expect VinUniversity Graduates to have:

  • The entrepreneurial mindset to recognize opportunities at local, regional, and global levels;
  • The entrepreneurial skills to empathize and evaluate customer needs with respect to economic and/or social impact;
  • The cooperative and leader mindset to work with multidisciplinary partners to develop creative solutions to unmet customer needs and other problems including social issues;
  • The entrepreneurial discipline to execute solutions for maximum impact.
